# 🎥 Final Coding & AI Video Project <!-- {docsify-ignore-all} -->

The BSMP26 Coding & AI Build Academy wraps up with a **Final Video Showcase Project** — your chance to put everything you've **Learned**, **Built**, and **Created** into one project and share it with mentors and peers.

<br>

---

## 🌟 What Is It?

Your capstone is a **5-minute video presentation** showcasing a complete web or AI application that you built. You can:

- **Build on a class assignment** — take a lesson project (your AI chatbot, agent, dashboard, or game) and make it your own, **or**
- **Create something new** — start from a fresh idea you're excited about.

Either way, your project should tell the story of what you built, how you used AI and GitHub Copilot to build it, and why it matters to you.

<br>

---

## 🧭 Project Requirements

> Your project should demonstrate the skills you developed across the program.

- ✅ A working **web or AI application** (web app, chatbot, agent, dashboard, game, or creative AI project)
- ✅ Built using **Python + GitHub Copilot** (agent mode) in **Codespaces**
- ✅ Uses at least one **AI capability** from class (e.g., AI chat, agents/tools, MCP, RAG, data insights, voice, or image generation)
- ✅ A **5-minute video** walking through your project — what it does, a live demo, and what you learned
- ✅ Code committed to your **GitHub repository**

?> **Advanced students:** aim to include a more advanced capability you explored — an MCP server, RAG over your own data, voice/image generation, or a multi-tool agent.

## 🎬 Tips for a Great Video

- **Start with the "why"** — what problem does your project solve, or what idea does it bring to life?
- **Show, don't just tell** — include a live demo of your app working.
- **Highlight the AI** — explain how you used Copilot, agents, or AI models to build it.
- **Keep it tight** — 5 minutes goes fast. Plan what you'll show.
- **Have fun with it** — your personality and creativity count!
